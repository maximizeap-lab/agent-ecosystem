# Platform Overview
### Coaching Dashboard — All Users

---

## What Is the Coaching Dashboard?

The Coaching Dashboard is an all-in-one platform that connects coaches and athletes in a shared digital workspace. It centralizes training plans, performance data, communication, and progress tracking so that every stakeholder has exactly the information they need — no spreadsheets, no scattered messages, no guesswork.

---

## Who Uses the Dashboard?

| Role | Primary Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Coach** | Design programs, monitor athlete progress, communicate feedback, manage a roster |
| **Athlete** | Follow training plans, log workouts, track progress, communicate with coaches |

---

## Core Feature Areas

### 1. 🏠 Home Dashboard
The central hub showing a personalized summary of today's activity, upcoming sessions, recent notifications, and quick-action buttons. Both roles land here after login, but the content displayed is role-specific.

### 2. 📋 Training Plans
- **Coaches** build, assign, and schedule structured training programs.
- **Athletes** view their current plan, see upcoming sessions, and access session details.
- Plans support daily, weekly, and block-based periodization.

### 3. 📊 Performance Tracking
- Log workouts, sets, reps, times, distances, and custom metrics.
- Auto-generated charts show trends over days, weeks, and months.
- Coaches see all athletes; athletes see only their own data.

### 4. 💬 Messaging & Feedback
- Direct one-on-one messaging between coach and athlete.
- Session-level comment threads for specific workout feedback.
- Group announcements from coach to the full roster.

### 5. 📅 Calendar
- Unified schedule showing training sessions, rest days, competitions, and events.
- Syncs with Google Calendar and Apple Calendar (optional).
- Coaches can push schedule updates that instantly appear on athlete calendars.

### 6. 📁 Media & Files
- Upload videos, images, and documents (technique videos, nutrition guides, etc.).
- Athletes can submit form-check videos for coach review.
- Storage organized by athlete, date, or category.

### 7. 📈 Reports & Analytics
- **Coaches:** roster-wide performance summaries, load monitoring, compliance rates.
- **Athletes:** personal progress reports, personal records (PRs), and readiness scores.

### 8. ⚙️ Settings
- Profile management, notification preferences, integrations, and billing.

## Data & Privacy at a Glance

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
- Athletes control what health data is visible to coaches via privacy settings.
- Data is never sold to third parties.
- Full GDPR and CCPA compliant.
